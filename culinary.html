Pretzels + Pretzel Bites

Taste fabulous. More of a fan of the bites - much simpler to make and taste better. Also the pretzels are too large, so they taste slightly doughy.

Garlic Knots

My personal favorite. Can be made with excess pizza dough and not very time consuming. Tastes far better than Olive Garden's btw.

Marshmallows

Fluffy and Heavenly Marshmallows. So one was selling these for $8 a marshmallow and I thought to myself I have to make these. Great dessert, but not worth the cleanup.

Potato Wedges

My go to recipe for a quick snack. Tastes great and takes only 15-30 minutes to make. But an airfryer is required.

Choclate Cake

This is the greatest Choclate Cake. It can go head to head against any bought cake. And best part is that it only takes about 3 hours, so I can make it whenever I want.

Swiss Rolls

Tastes Heavenly but so hard to make. You need a perfect sponge cake and have to roll in the frosting without the cake breaking. Very impressive nonetheless.
